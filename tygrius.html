Tygrius "Evertall" Thuliaga

The "Thuliaga" clan resided in the "Spine of the World" near "Reghed Glacier". We were a very tall tribe for Goliaths and I was the tallest of all, standing at eight foot, 2 inches. Being a mountain tribe, we faced many challenges but always found a way to live comfortably, until Barbarians of the Gray Wolf showed up.

We had seen tracks of their tribe before but never had they come so close to us. That night they were on the prowl looking for easy prey. The first scream was from the clan's "Tent-Mother"...my mother. Her scream mixed with the snarling of a dog.

I jumped out of my bed, grabbed my axe and shield, and ran out of my tent to see a lycanthrope gnawing at my mother's throat. I quickly looked around to find a pack of six lycans and many dead Goliath bodies. Other fighters came out and began to fight these lycans.

I ran to the one eating my mother and struck it down in one swift blow. Head and shoulder severed as I sliced the wolf in two. My mother was dead. I turned to see who else I would chop down. My tribesmen were dead and two other lycans still remained. I am not sure what happened next as I must have been blinded by the rage and anguish of seeing my mother torn apart by an animal. When I came to I was the only being still alive on that mountainside.

I buried every single body in that cold ground, lycan included. I travelled down that mountain towards Neverwinter, nothing left for me on that mountain except death and memories.
